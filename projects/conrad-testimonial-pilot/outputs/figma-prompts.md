# Design Direction Summary

## Selected Concept

CON-03: Benefit Validation Experience is the approved selected concept. The design exploration should help guests understand why Conrad Complete is credible, premium, and relevant by turning package inclusions into an evidence-backed decision experience.

CON-01: Editorial Feature Experience and CON-02: Guided Package Fit Experience are supporting influences only. Editorial recognition should support benefit validation, and fit guidance should preserve honest right-guest nuance without becoming a separate concept.

## Primary Exploration Direction

Benefit Evidence Matrix on the Conrad Complete Package Surface.

The primary exploration should organize Conrad Complete inclusions into decision-ready proof units that explain what is included, why it matters, what evidence supports it, and what terms or fit considerations keep the package story accurate.

## Secondary Exploration Direction

Editorial Proof Bridge supporting the Benefit Evidence Matrix, with Fit Guidance Panel as a subordinate support layer.

The secondary exploration should show how governed press recognition can strengthen trust near Club 66 and dining without becoming a generic testimonial, standalone hero, endorsement badge, ranking, or award claim.

## Primary Placement

Conrad Complete Package Surface, using the observed Summer Offers Conrad Complete module as the practical working surface until the canonical dedicated package surface is confirmed.

## Secondary Placement

Summer Offers / Offer Detail Surface, with the Club 66 Section as a supporting proof extension rather than a competing primary surface.

## Core Experience Pattern

Benefit Evidence Matrix.

## Supporting Patterns

- Governed editorial recognition moment.
- Lightweight fit guidance.
- Inclusion detail support.
- Source-aware proof bridge for Club 66 and dining.
- Terms-aware package qualification.
- Restrained booking confidence handoff.
