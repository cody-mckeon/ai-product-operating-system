# Design Direction Summary

## Selected Concept

CON-03: Benefit Validation Experience is the approved selected concept. The design direction should help guests understand why Conrad Complete is credible, premium, and relevant by turning its inclusions into an evidence-backed decision experience.

CON-01: Editorial Feature Experience and CON-02: Guided Package Fit Experience are approved only as supporting influences. They should not become separate competing explorations.

## Primary Exploration Direction

Benefit Evidence Matrix. Explore a package-surface structure that presents Conrad Complete benefits as decision-ready proof units. Each unit should clarify what is included, why it matters, what evidence supports it, and what terms or fit context keeps the claim accurate.

## Secondary Exploration Direction

Editorial Proof Bridge. Explore a governed press-recognition treatment that connects approved WSJ/source context to the benefits with the strongest evidence alignment, especially Club 66 and dining.

## Primary Placement

Conrad Complete Package Surface, including the observed Summer Offers Conrad Complete module until the canonical dedicated package surface is confirmed.

## Secondary Placement

Offer Detail Surface, with the Club 66 Section as a supporting deep-link or proof extension.

## Core Experience Pattern

Benefit Evidence Matrix.

## Supporting Patterns

- Governed editorial recognition moment.
- Lightweight fit guidance.
- Inclusion detail support.
- Source-aware proof bridge for Club 66 and dining.
- Terms-aware package qualification.
