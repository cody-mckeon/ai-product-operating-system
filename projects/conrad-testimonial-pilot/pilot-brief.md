# Conrad Testimonial Pilot

## Pilot Name

Conrad Testimonial Pilot

## Business Objective

Increase trust, decision confidence, and booking intent for Conrad Las Vegas and Conrad Complete package experiences.

## Background

A Wall Street Journal article, "My All-Inclusive Hotel Binge in Las Vegas," reviewed multiple all-inclusive experiences in Las Vegas and included favorable observations about the Conrad Complete experience at Resorts World Las Vegas.

The article represents third-party editorial validation from a recognized publication and may provide an opportunity to strengthen trust and credibility within Conrad-related experiences.

## Pilot Question

Should editorial validation from the Wall Street Journal be incorporated into Conrad experiences and, if so, where and how should it be presented?
