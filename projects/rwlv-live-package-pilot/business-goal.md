# Business Goal

## Primary Business Goals

- Increase qualified intent for entertainment-led resort packages.
- Improve attachment between live entertainment, hotel stays, dining, VIP experiences, and loyalty.
- Reduce planning friction for guests building a complete Resorts World visit around a show or event.
- Create a repeatable workflow for turning entertainment demand into measurable package opportunities.

## Secondary Goals

- Improve visibility of relevant resort offerings during entertainment discovery.
- Support higher-value bookings through curated room, dining, and access combinations.
- Create clearer handoffs between marketing, product, revenue, operations, design, analytics, and engineering.
- Build reusable AI-assisted prompts, scorecards, and telemetry structures for future package initiatives.
- Identify experiment opportunities that improve confidence, comparison, and booking intent.

## User Goals

- Understand what entertainment options are available and why they are worth planning around.
- Build a night, weekend, or special occasion with less effort.
- Compare packages by occasion, inclusions, schedule, price band, and level of access.
- Know what is included, what is optional, and what constraints apply.
- Move naturally from inspiration to availability, booking, reservation, or inquiry.
- Save, compare, or return to an experience without losing context.

## Measurable Outcomes

- Higher engagement with package modules and entertainment-led bundles.
- Increased package detail views from entertainment surfaces.
- Increased room availability checks that originate from entertainment context.
- Increased dining reservation starts or add-on interest from event journeys.
- Increased booking starts for packages or stay + event combinations.
- Improved package comparison behavior and lower abandonment after inclusion or pricing review.
- Better visibility into cross-sell, upsell, loyalty, and recovery paths.

## Success Metrics

- Package impression-to-detail rate.
- Package detail-to-availability-check rate.
- Inclusion expansion rate.
- CTA click-through rate by source module and journey stage.
- Room booking start rate from entertainment-led package paths.
- Ticket, room, dining, and package attachment rate.
- Package comparison interaction rate.
- Booking completion rate for package paths.
- Abandonment by funnel stage.
- Return visit and recovery conversion rate.
- Loyalty sign-in, join, or member offer engagement during package consideration.

## Guardrail Metrics

- Bounce or exit rate after package module exposure.
- CTA clicks that do not produce downstream intent.
- Abandonment after price, restriction, fee, account, availability, or third-party handoff exposure.
- Mobile dead clicks, repeated taps, and backtracking.
- Customer support or guest feedback indicating package confusion.
- Operational fulfillment exceptions caused by unclear package promises.
